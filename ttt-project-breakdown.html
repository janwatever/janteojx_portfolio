Trouble Through Town
Process Breakdown
A sheep herding game where you play as a Shepherd who needs to herd their sheep to safer pastures. Features
Herding sheep mechanic
Unique stressors in each area that causes sheep to panic and run away
Chase after runaway sheep and calm them back to the herd
4 uniquely designed areas
Stats Team Size : 5
Project Length:  3 months
Engine: Unity
Constraints
1 week to brainstorm a game and pitch it
11 weeks to fully design, develop and publish the game
2 mandatory playtest builds during the 3rd and 7th week of development 
It was the team's first attempt at creating a 3D game

Process

Brainstorm

Setup a team meeting to discuss, share and record ideas.

Design + Assign

Choose a set of proposed ideas that the team agrees with. If an idea fits within the current scope, assign implementation to whoever is interested and has time.
Otherwise attempt to design a better fit alternative.

Develop

Each person works on their assigned tasks individually and reach out to each other whenever help is needed.

Feedback + Loop

Once implemented, loop between getting feedback and polishing as needed. Some tasks might turn out to be much bigger than we initially anticipated after trying to develop it, so we would loop back to the drawing board.
