About Me

Born and raised in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. I'm currently a game design student in UE Berlin with experience working as a Character Modeler at Metronomik.

I love delving into multiple aspects of game development and particularly enjoy researching on how to achieve cool visual effects and mechanics.

Aside from videogames, I usually spend my time playing Pathfinder with friends and practice Taekwondo (Korean martial arts) on the weekends.
